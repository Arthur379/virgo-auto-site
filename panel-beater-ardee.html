Why Ardee drivers come to Virgo

Ardee is a traditional market town that sits where the N33 from Dundalk meets the N52 connecting Kells to the coast. It is a natural crossroads, and that means a lot of through-traffic — lorries, tractors, commuters heading in every direction. The narrow main street and tight junctions produce their share of scrapes and bumps, while the network of rural roads surrounding the town accounts for hedge damage, ditch drops and single-vehicle incidents that leave wings crumpled and doors out of alignment.

For a town its size, Ardee does not have a dedicated panel beating shop. The garages in the area are mostly mechanical — tyres, servicing, NCT prep — and panel work gets subcontracted out or handled with heavy filler. We offer an alternative. Because our Dundalk workshop is only 25 kilometres up the N33, Ardee is closer to us than many parts of Dundalk itself. The drive takes about 20 minutes, and we collect for free if your car cannot be driven.

We see a lot of cars from the Ardee area with damage typical of rural driving: low-speed impacts that bend rather than break, stone-chip craters on the leading edge of the bonnet, and sill damage from uneven farm entrances. These are exactly the type of repairs where proper panel beating saves money compared to wholesale panel replacement.

Services available to Ardee customers

Panel beating

Reshaping dented and deformed panels back to their original profile using traditional metalworking techniques.

Chassis alignment

Bench-mounted chassis correction with before-and-after jig measurements, documented for your records.

Structural repair

Welding and reinforcement of load-bearing panels, sills and subframe mounting points to factory spec.

Frame straightening

Heavy-pull bench work to correct chassis rails, A-pillars and bulkheads after serious collision damage.

Bumper & trim

Bumper straightening, bracket fabrication and trim refitting. Finished clean for your painter to take over.

Trade subcontract

Structural and panel work for Ardee-area garages and body shops. Trade pricing, quick turnaround, discreet.

From Ardee, head north on the N33 towards Dundalk. The road is direct and mostly dual carriageway, passing through Louth Village and Tallanstown before joining the outskirts of Dundalk. Frequently asked questions — Ardee

How long does it take to get from Ardee to Virgo Auto?

About 20 minutes by car. Take the N33 from Ardee towards Dundalk — it is a direct route with no motorway needed. If your car is not drivable after an accident, we offer free collection from Ardee and the surrounding villages.

Can you repair a car that was damaged on a country road near Ardee?

Yes. We regularly handle damage from rural road incidents — hedge scrapes, ditch drops, livestock encounters and single-vehicle impacts on narrow bends.
