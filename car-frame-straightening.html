What we can save

Insurance companies write cars off on economics, not on engineering. A car can be structurally sound after repair but still declared a write-off because the cost of fixing it through the insurer's approved repairer â€” at full labour rates, with new OEM parts, and a full respray â€” exceeds a threshold.
