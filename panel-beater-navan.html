Why Navan drivers come to Virgo

Navan is the county town and commercial centre of Meath, with a population that has grown substantially as a commuter hub for Dublin. The town sits at the junction of the N51 and the N3, with heavy traffic flowing between Dublin, Kells and the midlands. Congestion at roundabouts along the inner relief road and tight parking at the shopping centres in Navan town produce a regular crop of bumps, scrapes and low-speed collisions.

At 55 kilometres, Navan is just beyond our standard 50-kilometre free collection radius. But distance alone does not tell the full story. The drive from Navan to Dundalk takes about 45 minutes — head northeast on the N51 to Slane, then join the M1 northbound from junction 10. That is faster than battling into Dublin for many Navan drivers, and the cost of a repair at a specialist workshop with lower overheads often justifies the journey.

We are transparent about the distance. For larger jobs — chassis alignment, structural welding, multi-panel repairs — we will typically include collection at no extra charge because the work value makes it practical. For a single small dent, it may be more sensible for you to drop the car to us directly. Send photos and we will give you an honest assessment of whether the trip makes financial sense for your particular repair.

Services available to Navan customers

Panel beating

Skilled metalwork to bring dented and creased panels back to factory form without relying on heavy filler.

Chassis alignment

Jig-mounted correction and measurement — the kind of job worth travelling for if your local shop lacks the bench.

Structural repair

Load-bearing panels, sills, subframes and floor sections — welded, reinforced and finished to specification.

Frame straightening

Full bench pulls for heavily damaged chassis. Rails, strut towers and bulkhead alignment corrected and documented.

Bumper & trim

Reshaping, bracket fabrication and trim refit. Prepped to a clean standard ready for your chosen paint shop.

Trade subcontract

Panel and structural work for Meath garages and spray booths. Trade rates and quick return despite the distance.

Getting to us from Navan

From Navan, take the N51 east towards Slane. At Slane, join the M1 motorway northbound. Stay on the M1 for approximately 40 kilometres, then take junction 16 for Dundalk South. The total drive is about 55 kilometres and takes around 45 minutes. If you prefer to avoid Slane, the alternative is to take the N2 north from Slane towards Ardee and then continue to Dundalk. For cars that cannot be driven, contact us to discuss collection — we include it for free on most larger structural and chassis jobs from the Navan area.

Frequently asked questions — Navan

Navan is 55km away — do you still offer free collection?

Navan sits just outside our standard 50km free collection radius. For larger jobs — chassis alignment, structural work or multi-panel repairs — we will usually include collection at no extra charge. For smaller single-panel jobs, we can quote a nominal collection fee or you can drop the car to us. Either way, send photos first and we will work something out.

Why would a Navan driver go to Dundalk instead of Dublin for panel beating?

Dublin bodyshops carry Dublin overheads — higher rent, higher wages, higher insurance. Many also bundle painting and insurance admin into the price, inflating the total cost. We are a specialist metalwork-only shop with lower overheads in Dundalk. The journey from Navan takes about 45 minutes on the M1, which is quicker than driving into most Dublin industrial estates during peak hours. For structural or chassis work specifically, we have jig equipment that many Dublin bodyshops lack.
