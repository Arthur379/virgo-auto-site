Why Drogheda drivers come to Virgo

Drogheda has grown rapidly over the past two decades. With nearly 45,000 people, it is the biggest town in Louth and one of the busiest commuter hubs on the Dublin corridor. Traffic density means more prangs in supermarket car parks, more wing mirrors clipped on narrow streets around West Gate, and more rear-end shunts on the approach to the M1 interchange at Donore Road.

The challenge for Drogheda drivers who need panel work is finding a specialist. Most bodyshops in the town are full-service operations — painting, mechanical repairs, valeting — and panel beating is just one line item on a long menu. We take a different approach. Panel beating and structural metalwork is all we do. Because we focus on one discipline, we tend to produce cleaner results with less filler, less rework and lower costs than a generalist shop.

The journey from Drogheda to our Dundalk workshop is straightforward. Head north on the M1 motorway and take junction 16 for Dundalk South. The drive takes about 25 minutes in normal traffic. If you would rather not drive a damaged car, we will collect it from your home or workplace in Drogheda at no extra charge — it is well within our 50-kilometre radius.

Services available to Drogheda customers

Panel beating

Dents, creases and deformed panels reshaped to factory profile using hammer, dolly and shrinking techniques.

Chassis alignment

Jig-mounted straightening with documented measurements before and after. Brought within manufacturer tolerance.

Structural repair

Load-bearing sections, sills, subframes and structural welds restored to original specification.

Frame straightening

Full-body pulls on the bench for heavy collision damage. Chassis rails, A-pillars and bulkhead correction.

Bumper & trim

Bumper reshaping, bracket repair and trim refitting. Prepped clean and ready for your sprayer to finish.

Trade subcontract

Panel and structural work for Drogheda garages and sprayers. Trade rates, fast turnaround, your branding stays.
